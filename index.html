WHO WE ARE

Byron Dog Rescue aims to provide care and find homes for unwanted or surrendered dogs, rehabilitate orphaned, sick or injured dogs without owners, promote responsible pet ownership and provide information to the general public on animal welfare and the prevention of cruelty to animals. The journey for our furry little friends begin with the contribution of donations we receive to fund their health needs to get them up and going ready to be adopted into their forever homes. Support our cause today and help our little friends out because they deserve a second chance.

Donate to our lovely Byron dogs and give them an opportunity to live the life they deserve. We want to give second chances to our homeless companions and we need your help! When you contribute to our furry friends you begin their journey towards a healthy and fulfilling life. This is the first step towards being able to begin that journey in finding them the home they deserve.
